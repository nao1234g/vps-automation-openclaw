## 1. Stablecoin Law: Q1 2026 Deadline

South Korea's "Digital Asset Phase 2 legislation" is expected to be finalized in Q1 2026.

**The power struggle:**
- **Bank of Korea (BOK):** Only banks with 51%+ ownership should issue stablecoins
- **FSC (Financial Services Commission):** Don't lock out tech companies; that kills innovation

**Current requirements under draft:**
- Government authorization required
- 100% reserve backing mandatory
- User redemption rights guaranteed

**Signal:** Whoever wins this fight shapes the entire Korean stablecoin market. BOK victory = bank-dominated, FSC victory = fintech-friendly.
